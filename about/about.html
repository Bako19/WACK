ABOUT THE ARTIST

Camille Chiang is a contemporary visual artist born and raised in Hong Kong and now residing in San Francisco, Ca. Her portrait art focuses on capturing the subtle yet detailed emotions of human behavior using mixed media.
“My goal as an artist is to capture the small untold stories we all carry inside. I want to evoke those feelings and emotions that we often overlook or bury. Using mixed media, I work linearly from mess to precision, joining the abstract and surrealist art worlds to express vulnerability and identity.”
Camille moved to the United States with the ambition of starting a career in psychology, a field that relates closely to her artwork, but ended up venturing into the Web3 space. She was drawn to the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and art and to generative projects, a format requiring the artisst to relinquish elements of artistic control to AI to generate new creative forms. Four sold-out collections later, Camille is using her skills and Web3 journey as an avenue to create membership-based subscriptions to her work and releasing a curated collection of fashion items using her signature facial features, bright colors, and bold lines

WACK! is the official brand and company of the artist, Camille Chiang. WACK! encompasses all art, clothing, and products created by Camille, and home of her creativity.
