ABOUT THE ARTIST

Camille Chiang is a contemporary artist born and raised in Hong Kong and now residing in San Francisco. She focuses on capturing the detailed emotions of human behavior through a compelling series of mixed media portraits. Camille moved to the states with the ambition of starting a career in psychology - which is closly linked to her art; however ended up venturing into the web3 space whilst completing her education. Camille is now celebrating her 4th sold out collection, with a clothing brand underway.
